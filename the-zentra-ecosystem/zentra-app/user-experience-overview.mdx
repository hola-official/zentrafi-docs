## What is Zentra App?

Zentra App is ZentraFi's token launchpad interface: where projects create tokens and users participate in bonding curve trading before graduation to Zentra X.

Unlike traditional launchpads that require manual liquidity bootstrapping, Zentra App handles everything through a streamlined interface—tokens graduate automatically to concentrated liquidity pools.

The main dashboard displays:

- **Active Tokens**: Currently trading on bonding curves
- **Recently Graduated**: Tokens that moved to Zentra X
- **Your Holdings**: Tokens you've purchased

## For Participants

### Finding Tokens

**Browse Active Tokens:**
1. Navigate to "Tokens" tab
2. Filter by status (Active/Graduated)
3. Sort by market cap, age, or graduation progress

**Token Details Page:**
- Token information (name, symbol, supply)
- Current price and market cap
- Graduation progress (% of supply distributed)
- Social links and project information
- Contract addresses

### Buying Tokens

**Step 1: Review Token Details**

Before buying, check:
- [ ] Current price and market cap
- [ ] Graduation progress (how close to 20% remaining)
- [ ] Anti-sniper status (wait 30s after launch for best rates)
- [ ] Project legitimacy and socials

**Step 2: Connect Wallet**

Supported wallets:
- MetaMask
- WalletConnect
- Coinbase Wallet
- Other EVM-compatible wallets

**Step 3: Enter Amount**

- Input the native currency amount to spend
- Review estimated tokens at current price
- Note: Your purchase moves the price up (constant product AMM)

**Step 4: Confirm Transaction**

- Approve spending (first time only)
- Confirm buy transaction
- Wait for confirmation

<Warning>
**Anti-sniper protection:** Trades within 30 seconds of launch have a decaying tax (starts at 94%). Wait for the timer to expire for best prices.
</Warning>

### Selling Tokens

**Before Graduation:**
- You can sell tokens back to the bonding curve
- Selling moves the price down
- 2% sell fee applies (vs 1% buy fee)

**After Graduation:**
- Trade on Zentra X with concentrated liquidity
- Full ERC-20 transferability
- Standard DEX trading experience

### Token Restrictions

**Before graduation, tokens cannot be:**
- Transferred to other wallets
- Listed on external exchanges
- Added to external liquidity pools

This prevents manipulation and ensures orderly graduation.

### Graduation

When a token's reserve drops below 20% of supply:

1. Token automatically graduates
2. Transfer restrictions lift
3. sZTR holders receive 10% of raised funds
4. Remaining liquidity migrates to Zentra X
5. Trading continues on LiquidityBook

<Info>
Graduation happens automatically during a buy transaction when the threshold is reached. No manual action required.
</Info>

**Step 1: Configure Token**

```

Symbol: [PROJ]
Decimals: [18]
Description: [Your project description]
Image URL: [Logo image URL]
Twitter: [@yourproject]

```

**Step 2: Pay Launch Fee**

- Launch fee: 0.01 native tokens
- Prevents spam launches
- Token and pool deploy instantly

**Step 3: Token Goes Live**

Your token immediately:
- Has a bonding curve pool with virtual liquidity
- Can be bought/sold by anyone
- Starts earning creator fees (20% of trading fees)
- Has anti-sniper protection for 30 seconds

### Launch Economics

| Metric | Default Value |
|--------|---------------|
| Initial supply | 1,000,000,000 tokens |
| Initial market cap | ~$4,000 equivalent |
| Graduation threshold | 20% of tokens remaining |
| Graduation market cap | ~$100,000 equivalent |
| Capital to graduate | ~$16,000 equivalent |

As the token creator, you receive:

- **20% of trading fees** during bonding curve phase
- Fees distributed to your wallet automatically
- Tracked via `FeesDistributed` events

### Post-Graduation

After graduation:

1. **Automatic liquidity seeding**: 90% of raised funds go to Zentra X pool
2. **sZTR rewards**: 10% distributed to protocol stakers
3. **Trading continues**: Now on concentrated liquidity
4. **Your role**: Support community, build utility

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I buy multiple times?**
A: Yes. Each purchase is at the current price, which increases as supply decreases.

**Q: What if price changes while my transaction is pending?**
A: You'll receive tokens at the price when your transaction executes. Use slippage protection for large purchases.

**Q: Can I transfer tokens before graduation?**
A: No. Tokens are non-transferable until graduation. You can only sell back to the bonding curve.

**Q: What happens if graduation never occurs?**
A: You can always sell back to the pool. The bonding curve remains active indefinitely until graduation triggers.

**Q: How do I know when a token will graduate?**
A: Watch the "Tokens Remaining" counter. Graduation triggers at 20% (200M tokens remaining from 1B supply).
