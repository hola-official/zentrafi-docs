```

Zentra X:
```

## How It Works

### Discretized Liquidity

Instead of a continuous curve, liquidity exists at specific price points (bins):

```
Traditional AMM:
Price ──────────────────────────►
      ╲                        ╱
        ╲                    ╱     ← Your liquidity
          ╲                ╱         (infinitely thin)
            ────────────

Zentra X:
Price ──────────────────────────►
      [  ][  ][  ][██][  ][  ][  ]
                   ↑
             Active Bin          ← Your liquidity
            (concentrated)          (stacked deep)
```

### Bin Structure

Each bin holds liquidity at exactly one price:
- **Active bin**: Contains both tokens, earns all trading fees
- **Left of active**: Only quote token (ready to buy)
- **Right of active**: Only base token (ready to sell)

When trading exhausts the active bin, price moves to the next bin.

### Dynamic Fees

Fees automatically adjust based on market conditions:

$$
\text{Total Fee} = \text{Base Fee} + \text{Variable Fee}
$$

| Condition | Variable Fee | Why |
|-----------|--------------|-----|
| Low volatility | Near zero | Competitive rates attract volume |
| Moderate activity | Small addition | Slight LP compensation |
| High volatility | Significant spike | Hazard pay for LPs |
| Market chaos | Maximum | Full protection mode |
