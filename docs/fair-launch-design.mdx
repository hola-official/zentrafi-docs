## Post-Launch Liquidity Flow

After the launch:

- Liquidity is deployed to Zentra X
- DLMM manages liquidity placement
- Trading begins immediately

## Why DLMM Matters Here

DLMM helps absorb early volatility by:

- Concentrating liquidity near the active price
- Adjusting as market conditions change

This reduces extreme price swings common after launches.

## The Result

Tokens launched on ZentraFi don't just get listed — they get functional, efficient markets from day one.
