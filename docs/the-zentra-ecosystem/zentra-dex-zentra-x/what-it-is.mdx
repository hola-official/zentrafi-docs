## What is Zentra X?

Zentra X is ZentraFi's decentralized exchange: a trading platform built on the Liquidity Book model that enables efficient swaps with zero slippage within price bins.

Unlike traditional AMMs that spread liquidity across infinite prices, Zentra X concentrates capital in discrete bins, maximizing efficiency for traders and LPs alike.

## Key Characteristics

### Discretized Liquidity

Liquidity exists at specific price points, not across a curve:

```
Traditional AMM:
Price ──────────────────────────►
      ╲                        ╱
        ╲                    ╱
          ╲                ╱
            ────────────

Zentra X (Liquidity Book):
Price ──────────────────────────►
      [  ][  ][  ][██][  ][  ][  ]
                   ↑
             Active Bin
```

### Zero-Slippage Trading

Trades within a single bin execute at exactly the bin's price:
- Small trades: zero price impact
- Large trades: cross multiple bins at their respective prices

### Dynamic Fees

Fees respond to market conditions:
- Base fee: minimum trading cost
- Variable fee: increases with volatility
- Automatic surge pricing during high activity

### Capital Efficiency

LPs concentrate capital where it's used:
- No idle liquidity at unrealistic prices
- Higher fee capture per dollar deposited
- Better returns for active capital

## How Zentra X Differs

### vs. Constant Product AMMs (Uniswap v2)

| Aspect | Uniswap v2 | Zentra X |
|--------|------------|----------|
| Price curve | $xy=k$ | Discrete bins |
| Slippage | Every trade | Zero in-bin |
| LP positions | Fungible | Per-bin shares |
| Capital efficiency | ~2-5% | Up to 100% |
| Fees | Fixed 0.3% | Dynamic |

### vs. Concentrated Liquidity (Uniswap v3)

| Aspect | Uniswap v3 | Zentra X |
|--------|------------|----------|
| Price representation | Ticks on curve | Discrete bins |
| Position tracking | NFTs | Semi-fungible |
| Out-of-range behavior | No fees | Clear bin state |
| Complexity | High | Medium |
| Fee model | Fixed tiers | Dynamic |

### vs. Order Books

| Aspect | Order Book | Zentra X |
|--------|------------|----------|
| Liquidity type | Limit orders | Pooled bins |
| Price discovery | Bid/ask spread | Active bin |
| Execution | Match orders | Swap against pool |
| Capital requirement | Per-order | Pooled |

## Supported Operations

### For Traders

- **Market swaps**: Trade at current market price
- **Multi-hop routing**: Path through multiple pools
- **Price quotes**: Preview execution before trading
- **Slippage protection**: Set maximum acceptable slippage

### For LPs

- **Add liquidity**: Deposit to specific bins
- **Remove liquidity**: Withdraw from positions
- **Claim fees**: Collect accumulated trading fees
- **Rebalance**: Adjust positions as price moves

### For Builders

- **Pool queries**: Read pool state and pricing
- **Swap integration**: Execute swaps from contracts
- **LP automation**: Programmatic position management
- **Analytics**: Historical data and metrics
