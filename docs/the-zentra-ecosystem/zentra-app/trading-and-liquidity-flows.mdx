## Overview

Zentra App manages the transition from token launch to open market trading.

This page explains how liquidity flows through the system.

## Pre-Launch

- Project configures token and launch parameters
- Launch is announced and visible to participants
- No trading occurs during this phase

## During Launch

- Participants contribute to the launch
- Price adjusts based on demand (bonding curve)
- Tokens are allocated proportionally
- Liquidity accumulates for post-launch deployment

## Launch Completion

When the launch ends:

1. Final token price is determined
2. Tokens are distributed to participants
3. Liquidity is prepared for Zentra X

## Liquidity Deployment

After launch completion:

- Liquidity is deployed to DLMM pools on Zentra X
- Bins are configured around the launch price
- Trading is enabled immediately

## Active Trading

Once live on Zentra X:

- Users can swap tokens freely
- LPs can add additional liquidity
- Dynamic fees adjust based on market activity
- The DLMM engine manages bin liquidity

## Flow Diagram

```
Participants → Launch Pool → Token Distribution
                    ↓
              Liquidity Pool
                    ↓
              Zentra X (DLMM)
                    ↓
              Open Trading
```

## Key Points

- No manual liquidity bootstrapping required
- Seamless transition from launch to trade
- DLMM ensures efficient price discovery post-launch
