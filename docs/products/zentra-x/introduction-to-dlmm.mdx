---
title: "Introduction to DLMM"
description: "Zentra X's Dynamic Liquidity Market Maker (DLMM) gives liquidity providers access to dynamic fees and precise liquidity concentration all in real-time."
---

DLMM provides liquidity providers (LPs) with enhanced flexibility and fee optimization through its advanced feature set.

Inspired by Trader Joe's Liquidity Book, DLMM organizes asset pair liquidity into discrete price bins. Each bin contains token reserves available for exchange at a specific price point, enabling swaps within a bin to execute without slippage. The market for an asset pair is formed by aggregating these discrete liquidity bins.

LPs earn revenue through three mechanisms: standard liquidity provision fees, dynamic fees during periods of market volatility, and Liquidity Mining (LM) rewards where available.

DLMM also powers the DLMM Launch Pool, a specialized pool type for new token launches. This pool comes equipped with features optimized for bootstrapping liquidity and ensuring token accessibility on Jupiter and other trading platforms.
