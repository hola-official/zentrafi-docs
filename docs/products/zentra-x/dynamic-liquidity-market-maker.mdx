## Overview

Liquidity provision on Zentra X uses the Liquidity Book model. You allocate capital across specific price bins rather than a single pool position.

## How It Works

### Bin-Based Positions

When you provide liquidity, you deposit into price bins:

```
Your Position:
├── Bin 8388606 ($0.99): 500 USDC
├── Bin 8388608 ($1.00): 1000 USDC  ← Active bin
├── Bin 8388610 ($1.01): 500 USDC
└── Total: 2000 USDC
```

### Token Composition

| Bin Location | You Deposit | Why |
|--------------|-------------|-----|
| Below active | Token Y only | Bins "sold out" of X |
| Active bin | Both tokens | Mixed |
| Above active | Token X only | Bins "sold out" of Y |

### Fee Earning

Only the **active bin** earns fees. Your earnings depend on:
- Your share of the active bin
- Trading volume
- Current fee level (dynamic)

## Adding Liquidity

1. **Select Pool**: Choose token pair, review stats
2. **Choose Shape**: [Select distribution strategy](/docs/products/zentra-x/liquidity-shapes)
3. **Set Range**: How many bins to cover
4. **Deposit**: Approve and confirm transaction

## Managing Positions

**Monitor:**
- Current value and fees earned
- In-range status
- Bin distribution

**Rebalance when:**
- Position mostly out of range
- IL exceeds fee earnings
- Market conditions changed

**Claim fees:**
- No lockup, claim anytime
- Paid in LP tokens

## Risk Factors

### Impermanent Loss

- **Within bin**: No IL (constant sum formula)
- **Across bins**: Discrete IL when bins flip

### Concentration Trade-off

| Width | Fee Potential | IL Risk | Management |
|-------|---------------|---------|------------|
| Wide | Lower | Lower | Passive |
| Medium | Moderate | Moderate | Occasional |
| Tight | Higher | Higher | Active |

## Quick Tips

<Warning>
**Common mistakes to avoid:**
- Too concentrated without monitoring
- Ignoring IL during trends
- Over-trading (gas costs add up)
</Warning>
