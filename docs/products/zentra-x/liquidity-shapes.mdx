## Overview

When providing liquidity on Zentra X, you choose how to distribute capital across price bins. Each shape has different risk/reward characteristics.

## Shapes

### Spot (Uniform)

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/spot-shape.png" alt="Spot Distribution" style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />
</Frame>

Equal liquidity across all selected bins.

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Distribution | Uniform |
| Best for | Ranging markets, passive LPs, uncertain direction |
| Risk level | Medium |
| Rebalancing | Low frequency |

**When to use:**
- You're new to LP
- Market has no clear trend
- You want less active management

---

### Curve (Concentrated)

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/curve-shape.png" alt="Curve Distribution" style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />
</Frame>

Heavy allocation at current price, tapering outward.

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Distribution | Bell curve |
| Best for | Stable pairs, high conviction, max fee capture |
| Risk level | Higher |
| Rebalancing | Frequent |

**When to use:**
- Stablecoin pairs (USDC/USDT)
- You expect price to stay in tight range
- You want maximum capital efficiency

---

### Bid-Ask

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/bid-ask-shape.png" alt="Bid-Ask Distribution" style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />
</Frame>

Capital on both ends with gap around current price.

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Distribution | Inverted curve |
| Best for | Volatile pairs, DCA strategies, capturing swings |
| Risk level | Lower IL risk |
| Rebalancing | Moderate |

**When to use:**
- You expect large price swings
- Natural dollar-cost averaging
- Mean reversion strategies

## Spot Variations

For more granular control, Spot shape has concentration options:

| Variation | Bins | Use Case | Management |
|-----------|------|----------|------------|
| **Concentrated** | 1-3 | Stables, max efficiency | Very active |
| **Normal** | 10-20 | Standard pairs | Daily check |
| **Wide** | 50+ | Volatile pairs, hands-off | Passive |

## Choosing a Shape

```
Is the pair stable (e.g., USDC/USDT)?
├── Yes → Curve (tight)
└── No
    ├── Do you expect a trend?
    │   ├── Yes → Single-sided or Bid-Ask
    │   └── No → Spot
    └── High volatility expected?
        ├── Yes → Spot (wide) or Bid-Ask
        └── No → Spot (normal) or Curve
```

## Bin Step Selection

Bin step determines price granularity:

| Bin Step | Price Increment | Best For |
|----------|-----------------|----------|
| 1 bp | 0.01% | Stablecoins |
| 10 bp | 0.1% | Major pairs |
| 25 bp | 0.25% | Standard tokens |
| 100 bp | 1% | Volatile/new tokens |

Smaller steps = more precision, but narrower max range per position.
