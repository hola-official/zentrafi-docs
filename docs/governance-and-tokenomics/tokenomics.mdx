## ZTR Token

ZTR is ZentraFi's native protocol token.

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Name | Zentra |
| Symbol | ZTR |
| Chain | Pharos |
| Standard | ERC-20 |

## Token Utility

ZTR has three primary functions within the protocol:

### 1. Governance

Stake ZTR to participate in protocol governance:

- Accrue veZTR voting power
- Direct liquidity emissions
- Vote on protocol parameters
- Influence treasury decisions

### 2. Revenue Sharing

Stakers earn sZTR: a share of protocol revenue:

- Trading fees from Zentra X
- Launch fees from Zentra App
- Protocol fees (where applicable)

This creates real yield from actual protocol activity.

### 3. Incentive Alignment

ZTR aligns ecosystem participants:

- LPs earn ZTR for providing liquidity
- Projects can use voting rewards
- Long-term stakers have more influence
- Active participation is rewarded

## Economic Model

### Fee Structure

#### Zentra X Trading Fees

| Pool Type | Base Fee | Variable Fee |
|-----------|----------|--------------|
| Stable | 0.02-0.05% | Low |
| Standard | 0.1-0.3% | Medium |
| Volatile | 0.3-0.8% | High |

Variable fees increase with volatility (surge pricing).

#### Protocol Fee Share

A portion of trading fees flows to the protocol:

- Configurable by governance
- Typically 10-20% of LP fees
- Distributed to sZTR stakers and treasury

#### Launch Fees

Token launches may include:
- Platform fee on raise
- Configurable by launch creator
- Flows to protocol treasury

### Emission Model

ZTR emissions fund liquidity incentives:

```
Total Emissions
       │
       ├──► Liquidity Mining (largest portion)
       │
       ├──► Staking Rewards
       │
       └──► Ecosystem/Treasury
```

**Emission Schedule:**
- Defined total allocation
- Distributed over emission period
- Rate may decay over time
- Governance-adjustable parameters

### Sustainability Mechanics

The model is designed for long-term sustainability:

**Real Yield Foundation:**
- Revenue comes from actual trading activity
- Not dependent on token emissions
- Scales with protocol usage

**Emission Efficiency:**
- Gauge voting directs emissions productively
- Unproductive pools lose rewards
- Market determines optimal allocation

**Alignment Mechanisms:**
- veZTR rewards long-term commitment
- Unstaking penalty (veZTR reset) discourages mercenary behavior
- Voting rewards create positive-sum games
