## ZTR Token

ZTR is the native token of the ZentraFi protocol.

**Chain:** Pharos
**Token Type:** Governance & Utility

## Token Utility

The ZTR token serves multiple functions within the ecosystem:

### Governance

- Stake ZTR to accrue veZTR voting power
- Direct protocol emissions to liquidity pools
- Vote on protocol parameters and treasury usage

### Revenue Sharing

- Stake ZTR to earn sZTR rewards
- Receive share of protocol trading fees
- Real yield from actual platform activity

### Liquidity Incentives

- Farm rewards distributed in ZTR
- DLMM incentives for active liquidity
- Voting rewards for governance participants

### Fee Structure

Zentra X collects trading fees from all swaps:

- **DLMM Pools:** Dynamic fees (0.02% - 2%+ during volatility)
- **Standard Pools:** Fixed percentage fees

A portion of fees goes to:

- Liquidity providers (majority)
- Protocol treasury
- sZTR stakers (revenue share)

## Emission Model

ZTR emissions are distributed over a defined period:

- **Liquidity Mining:** Rewards for LP stakers
- **Staking Rewards:** sZTR distributions
- **Ecosystem Growth:** Treasury-funded initiatives

Emissions are directed by veZTR holders through gauge voting.

<Warning>
Emission rates and allocations are subject to governance decisions. Details will be finalized before launch.
</Warning>

## Economic Principles

ZentraFi tokenomics are designed around:

- **Real yield** — Revenue from actual trading activity, not just emissions
- **Long-term alignment** — veZTR mechanics reward committed participants
- **Capital efficiency** — DLMM ensures liquidity is used productively
- **Sustainability** — Fee capture creates ongoing value for token holders
