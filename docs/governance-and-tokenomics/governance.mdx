## Overview

ZentraFi is governed by token holders through a vote-escrowed governance system.

Users stake ZTR tokens to receive **voting power (veZTR)** and **real yield (sZTR)** simultaneously.

## Staking

Staking ZTR is the foundation of ZentraFi governance.

### What You Get

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **sZTR** | Share of protocol trading fees (real yield) |
| **veZTR** | Voting power to direct emissions |

### How It Works

- Stake ZTR tokens at any time
- No deposit or withdrawal fees
- No lock-up periods — unstake whenever you want
- Staking is unified: you earn both sZTR and veZTR automatically

### sZTR: Revenue Sharing

sZTR provides real yield derived from platform trading fees:

- Fees are collected regularly from Zentra X
- Converted to ZTR and distributed to stakers
- Rewards proportional to your share of total staked ZTR

**Reward Calculation:**
```
Your Reward Rate = (Your Staked ZTR / Total Staked ZTR) × sZTR Reward Rate
```

## veZTR: Voting Power

veZTR is a non-transferable token representing your voting power within the protocol.

### Accrual Mechanics

- **Maximum veZTR:** 1000× your staked ZTR balance
- **Accrual rate:** Linear, reaching maximum in 7 days
- **Unstaking:** Resets your entire veZTR balance to zero

<Warning>
Unstaking any amount of ZTR will result in the loss of all accrued veZTR. You must also remove all active votes before unstaking.
</Warning>

### Topping Up

Adding more ZTR to your stake:

- Automatically claims pending sZTR rewards
- Increases your veZTR maximum
- New deposits begin accruing veZTR over 7 days

## Gauges & Voting

Gauges measure and distribute rewards to liquidity pools based on veZTR holder votes.

### How Voting Works

1. Stake ZTR and accrue veZTR
2. Direct your veZTR votes to approved pools
3. Pools with more votes receive higher ZTR emissions
4. LPs in those pools earn more rewards

### Voting Power Model

ZentraFi uses a power model for counting votes:

```
Pool Weight = votes^(2/3)
Emission Share = Pool Weight / Total Weight
```

This encourages broader participation across multiple pools rather than concentration on single options.

### Key Rules

- Split votes across multiple pools
- Withdraw and redirect votes anytime
- Changes take effect immediately
- Remove all votes before unstaking

## Voting Rewards

A permissionless incentive layer where anyone can deposit tokens to reward voters.

### How It Works

1. Projects or users create Voting Reward Pools
2. They deposit incentive tokens for a specific LP pool
3. veZTR holders opt-in to earn rewards
4. Rewards distributed proportionally to votes

This creates additional yield opportunities beyond farm emissions — projects can incentivize liquidity by rewarding voters directly.

<Info>
Voting alone doesn't qualify you for rewards. You must opt-in to specific Voting Reward Pools.
</Info>

## What Governance Controls

veZTR holders can vote on:

- Liquidity incentives distribution
- Supported markets and pools
- Emission allocation
- Treasury usage
- Protocol parameters

Governance is designed to reward **long-term alignment**, not short-term speculation.
