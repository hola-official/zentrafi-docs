## Overview

Farming on ZentraFi allows you to generate additional yield on your pooled liquidity.

Certain liquidity pools receive farm reward emissions governed by ZTR stakers through veZTR voting, making yields dynamic based on voting activity.

## How Farming Works

### Step 1: Provide Liquidity

1. Deposit tokens into a liquidity pool on Zentra X
2. Receive LP (liquidity provider) tokens as a receipt
3. These LP tokens represent your share of the pool

### Step 2: Stake LP Tokens

1. Navigate to the Farm page
2. Locate your desired farm
3. Enter the LP token amount you wish to stake
4. Approve and confirm the transaction

### Step 3: Earn Rewards

- Rewards accrue automatically while staked
- Claim rewards at any time
- Rewards are added directly to your wallet

## Unstaking

1. Use the Unstake tab to select your LP token amount
2. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
3. Visit the pool page to withdraw your original tokens from liquidity

## Key Features

- **Dynamic emissions** — Reward rates change based on veZTR voting
- **No lock-ups** — Stake or unstake at any time
- **Flexible claiming** — Harvest rewards whenever you want
- **Multiple farms** — Manage positions across different pools

## Farm Rewards

Farm emissions are directed by veZTR holders through the gauge voting system.

Pools with more votes receive higher ZTR emissions, creating an incentive for:

- Projects to attract votes to their pools
- LPs to stake in high-emission farms
- veZTR holders to participate in governance

<Warning>
Farm APRs fluctuate based on voting activity and total staked liquidity. Past performance does not guarantee future returns.
</Warning>

## DLMM Farms

For Liquidity Book (DLMM) pools, rewards work differently:

- No need to stake LP tokens separately
- ZTR rewards accrue automatically when liquidity is in range
- Rewards are claimable at any time
- Only active liquidity (within the price range) earns rewards

This makes DLMM farming more capital efficient — you earn rewards only when your liquidity is actually being used.
