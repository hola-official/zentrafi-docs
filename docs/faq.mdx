## Trading on Zentra X

### I swapped tokens but can't see them in my wallet

You may need to manually add the token's contract address to your wallet:
1. Find the token's contract address on the pool or token page
2. In your wallet, select "Import Token" or "Add Custom Token"
3. Paste the contract address and confirm

### My swap failed or was reverted

During high volatility, transactions may revert due to price changes between bins. Try:
- **Increase slippage tolerance** — Use the settings icon to adjust
- **Reduce trade size** — Large trades cross multiple bins and are more likely to revert
- **Retry in a few minutes** — Wait for volatility to settle

<Info>
Tokens with buy/sell taxes may require higher slippage (up to 20% depending on the token's fee structure).
</Info>

### Why did I receive slightly fewer tokens than expected?

Zentra X uses dynamic fees that increase during volatile periods:
- **Base fee**: Minimum trading cost
- **Variable fee**: Increases when many bins are crossed or during high activity

This "surge pricing" compensates LPs for increased risk during volatility.

### A failed transaction took my tokens

Failed transactions only deduct gas fees. All other tokens revert to your wallet. If you believe tokens are missing:
1. Check your transaction history on the block explorer
2. Verify the token is added to your wallet
3. Contact support via [Discord](https://linktr.ee/zentrafi) if issues persist

## Providing Liquidity (DLMM)

### Why am I not earning fees?

Only the **active bin** earns trading fees. If price has moved away from your position:
- Your liquidity is "out of range"
- You won't earn fees until price returns to your bins
- Consider rebalancing your position closer to the current price

### What are the different liquidity shapes?

| Shape | Description | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Spot** | Uniform distribution across bins | Stable pairs, passive LPs |
| **Curve** | Concentrated around current price | Active management, higher fees |
| **Bid-Ask** | Split on either side of price | Market making strategies |

See [Liquidity Shapes](/docs/products/zentra-x/liquidity-shapes) for details.

### My LP position shows a small amount

LP shares are proportional to total pool liquidity. With large pools, individual shares appear minimal but still represent your actual ownership percentage.

### How do I know if I'm earning fees?

- Fees accrue directly to your position in the active bin
- Check your position value over time
- Use portfolio trackers to monitor gains
- Compare your position value when removing liquidity vs. when you added

### What is impermanent loss in DLMM?

When price moves significantly:
- Your position composition changes (more of one token, less of the other)
- If price moves away and doesn't return, you may have less value than simply holding

More concentrated positions = higher fees when in range, but higher impermanent loss risk.

## Zentra App (Launchpad)

### I can't find a launch

- Check all tabs: **Active**, **Upcoming**, and **Completed**
- Verify the launch hasn't ended
- Some launches may have participation requirements

### I participated but can't claim my tokens

Tokens are claimable only **after the launch completes**:
1. Wait for the launch to finalize
2. Navigate to "Your Launches" or the launch page
3. Click "Claim Tokens"
4. Confirm the transaction

### The launch failed — how do I get a refund?

If a launch doesn't reach its soft cap:
1. Launch is marked as "Failed"
2. Go to the launch page
3. Click "Claim Refund"
4. Your full contribution is returned

### Why did my token price change during purchase?

Zentra App uses **adaptive pricing** (bonding curves):
- Price increases as more people participate
- Your final token amount depends on the price when your transaction executes
- This is normal and ensures fair price discovery

## Token Locker

### What's the difference between Standard and Vesting locks?

| Type | How it works |
|------|--------------|
| **Standard** | All tokens unlock on a single date |
| **Vesting** | Tokens unlock gradually (TGE + periodic cycles) |

### Why is there a 1% unlock fee?

The 1% fee is charged when unlocking tokens (not when creating locks). This fee supports protocol operations.

### Can I modify my lock?

You can:
- ✅ Extend the unlock date
- ✅ Increase the locked amount
- ✅ Transfer ownership to another wallet
- ✅ Renounce ownership (permanent)

You cannot:
- ❌ Reduce the locked amount
- ❌ Shorten the unlock date

### My veZTR reset to zero — what happened?

If you're staking ZTR for veZTR:
- **Unstaking any amount resets your entire veZTR balance to zero**
- veZTR takes 7 days to reach maximum (1000x)
- This is by design to reward long-term stakers

## Airdrop Tool

### My airdrop transaction failed

Common causes:
- **Insufficient balance** — Ensure you have enough tokens + gas + service fee
- **Too many recipients** — Maximum 1,000 recipients per transaction
- **Token approval** — Make sure you've approved the total airdrop amount

### Can I undo an airdrop?

<Warning>
Airdrops are irreversible. Once sent, tokens cannot be recovered if sent to wrong addresses. Always double-check recipient addresses before confirming.
</Warning>

## Wallet & General Issues

### I sent tokens to a contract address

Tokens sent directly to contract addresses are typically **unrecoverable**. Contracts aren't designed to receive direct sends. Always use the proper interface (Zentra X, Zentra App) to interact with the protocol.

### My tokens/positions disappeared

1. Check your transaction history on the block explorer
2. Verify you're connected to the correct wallet
3. Ensure you're on the correct network (Pharos)
4. If tokens transferred to unknown wallets, your wallet may be compromised

<Warning>
**Security tips:**
- Use hardware wallets for large amounts
- Never share your seed phrase or private keys
- Regularly review and revoke unnecessary token approvals
- Be cautious of phishing sites — always verify you're on zentrafi.xyz
</Warning>

### The site isn't loading properly

Try these steps:
1. Clear browser cache and site data
2. Disable conflicting browser extensions (especially multiple wallet extensions)
3. Try a different browser
4. Check if you have ad blockers interfering
