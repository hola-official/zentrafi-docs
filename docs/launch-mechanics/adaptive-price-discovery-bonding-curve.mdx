## Launch Lifecycle

1. Project configures launch parameters
2. Users participate in token distribution
3. Price adjusts dynamically based on demand
4. Launch completes and liquidity is created
5. Token becomes tradable on Zentra X

This creates a smooth transition from launch to open trading.

## Why Adaptive Pricing?

Fixed-price launches often lead to:

- Bots dominating allocation
- Immediate price spikes
- Rapid sell-offs

Adaptive pricing responds to real participation levels.

## Price Dynamics

- Early participation starts at a lower price
- Increased demand pushes the price upward
- The final price reflects actual interest

This improves fairness and stability.
