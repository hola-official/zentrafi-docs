## What is ZentraFi?

ZentraFi is a vertically integrated DeFi protocol built natively on **Pharos**. It unifies two critical pieces of on-chain infrastructure:

1. **Zentra App** — A programmable launchpad with adaptive price discovery
2. **Zentra X** — A discretized liquidity exchange powered by the Liquidity Book model

Together, these components solve a fundamental problem in DeFi: the disconnect between token distribution and market formation.

## The Problem with Isolated Infrastructure

Traditional DeFi separates token launches from trading infrastructure:

| Phase | Traditional Approach | What Goes Wrong |
|-------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Launch | Fixed-price sale or bonding curve | Bots front-run, retail gets poor fills |
| Liquidity | Manual LP bootstrapping | Fragmented, often mercenary capital |
| Trading | Generic AMM listing | Inefficient pricing, high slippage |

This fragmentation creates friction at every step. Projects spend resources on liquidity mining. Traders deal with poor execution. LPs face impermanent loss in poorly designed pools.

## How ZentraFi Works Differently

ZentraFi treats launch and trade as a single continuous flow:

```
Token Configuration → Adaptive Price Discovery → Liquidity Formation → Discretized Trading
```

### Launch Phase

Projects configure distribution parameters — supply, pricing curves, duration. Participants buy in through adaptive pricing that responds to demand in real-time. No fixed allocations to game. No cliff unlocks to dump.

### Market Formation

When distribution completes, raised capital doesn't sit idle. It flows directly into Zentra X as concentrated liquidity, seeded into bins around the terminal price. The token has a market from minute one.

### Ongoing Trading

Zentra X handles all subsequent trading through the Liquidity Book model. Price moves discretely across bins. Fees adjust dynamically. LPs earn on active capital, not idle reserves.

## Technical Foundation

ZentraFi's exchange layer implements the **Liquidity Book** model — a discretized approach to automated market making that differs fundamentally from constant-product designs.

### Bin-Based Liquidity

Instead of continuous curves, liquidity exists in discrete price bins. Each bin:

- Holds reserves at a single price point
- Executes swaps at constant price until depleted
- Enables zero-slippage trades within the bin

### Dynamic Fee Structure

Fees consist of two components:

$$
\text{Total Fee} = \text{Base Fee} + \text{Variable Fee}
$$

The variable component responds to volatility — measured by bin crossings and swap frequency. High activity periods generate higher fees, compensating LPs for increased risk and discouraging extractive behavior.

### Capital Efficiency

Traditional AMMs spread liquidity across infinite price ranges. Most of it sits unused. Liquidity Book concentrates capital where trading actually occurs:

| Metric | Constant Product AMM | Liquidity Book |
|--------|---------------------|----------------|
| Active Capital | ~2-5% | Up to 100% |
| Slippage | Continuous | Zero within bins |
| Fee Capture | Diluted | Concentrated |

ZentraFi operates across three layers:

**Application Layer**
- User interfaces for launching, trading, and LP management
- Wallet integration and transaction signing
- Real-time market data and position tracking

**Protocol Layer**
- Launchpad contracts for token distribution
- Liquidity Book pools for trading
- Router contracts for optimal execution

**Governance Layer**
- veZTR voting for emission direction
- Treasury management through governance proposals
- Protocol parameter adjustment
