## Key Features

- **Zero Slippage**: Trade at exact bin prices
- **Dynamic Fees**: Adjust automatically with volatility
- **Launch-to-Trade**: Token sales seed DLMM pools atomically
- **Concentrated LP**: Deploy capital where trading happens
