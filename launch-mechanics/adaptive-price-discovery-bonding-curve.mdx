## Overview

ZentraFi's launchpad uses a **Uniswap V2-style constant product AMM** with virtual liquidity to enable fair, transparent token launches. Tokens trade against WPROS (Wrapped PROS) and automatically graduate to TraderJoe's LiquidityBook when distribution thresholds are met.

## The Constant Product Model

Unlike traditional bonding curves with linear or exponential functions, ZentraFi uses the proven constant product formula:

$$
x \times y = k
$$

Where:
- $x$ = Token reserve
- $y$ = Quote reserve (virtual + real WPROS)
- $k$ = Constant product

This creates predictable pricing where buying tokens increases price and selling decreases it.

### Price Calculation

$$
\text{Price} = \frac{\text{Quote Reserve}}{\text{Token Reserve}}
$$

$$
\text{Market Cap} = \text{Price} \times \text{Total Supply}
$$

## Virtual Liquidity

The key innovation is **virtual liquidity**: the pool starts with simulated quote reserves that create immediate price discovery without requiring upfront capital.

### How It Works

Virtual quote reserves are calculated using two parameters:

| Parameter | Description | Default |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| `K` | Base curve constant | 100,000,000,000 (100B) |
| `assetRate` | Rate to maintain target USD market cap | 5,000,000,000 |

**Formula:**

$$
\text{Virtual Quote} = \frac{K \times \text{ASSET\_RATE\_PRECISION}}{\text{assetRate} \times 10^9}
$$

With default settings (K=100B, assetRate=5B):
- **Initial market cap**: 200 WPROS (~$4,000 at $20/WPROS)
- **Capital to graduate**: 800 WPROS (~$16,000)
- **Graduation market cap**: 5,000 WPROS (~$100,000)

### Adjusting for Quote Asset Price

The `assetRate` parameter allows maintaining consistent USD-denominated market caps when WPROS price changes:

| WPROS Price | Target Virtual (WPROS) | assetRate |
|-------------|------------------------|-----------|
| $20.00 | 200 | 5,000,000,000 (default) |
| $10.00 | 400 | 2,500,000,000 |
| $40.00 | 100 | 10,000,000,000 |
| $1.00 | 4,000 | 250,000,000 |

**Rule of thumb:** When WPROS price doubles, double the `assetRate` to maintain the same USD market cap.

## Graduation Mechanics

Tokens **graduate** to TraderJoe LiquidityBook when the pool token reserve drops below the graduation threshold.

### Graduation Threshold

| Parameter | Default | Description |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| `graduationThresholdBps` | 2000 (20%) | Triggers when token reserve falls below this % |

With 20% threshold:
- Launch starts with 1,000,000,000 tokens in pool
- Graduation triggers at 200,000,000 tokens remaining
- 80% of supply has been distributed

### Graduation Economics

The relationship between threshold and outcomes is fixed:

$$
r = \frac{10000}{\text{Threshold BPS}}
$$

| Threshold | r | Tokens Sold | Price Multiplier | MCap/Capital Ratio |
|-----------|---|-------------|------------------|-------------------|
| 20% | 5 | 80% | 25x | 6.25 |
| 25% | 4 | 75% | 16x | 5.33 |
| 30% | 3.33 | 70% | 11x | 4.76 |
| 50% | 2 | 50% | 4x | 4.0 |

**Key formulas:**
- Initial Market Cap = V (virtual liquidity)
- Capital to Graduate = V × (r - 1)
- Graduation Market Cap = V × r²

### Example: Default Configuration

| Stage | Token Reserve | Quote Reserve | Price (WPROS) | Price (USD) | Market Cap |
|-------|---------------|---------------|---------------|-------------|------------|
| Launch | 1,000,000,000 | 200 | 0.0000002 | $0.000004 | $4,000 |
| Graduation | 200,000,000 | 1,000 | 0.000005 | $0.0001 | $100,000 |

- **Capital required**: 800 WPROS (~$16,000)
- **Price multiplier**: 25x from launch to graduation

## Graduation Process

When graduation triggers:

```
1. Liquidity removed from V2-style pair
2. Token becomes fully transferable
3. sZTR holders receive allocation (10% of raised WPROS)
4. Remaining liquidity migrates to LiquidityBook
5. Concentrated liquidity distributed across bins
```

### sZTR Holder Rewards

| Parameter | Default | Description |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| `sZTRAllocationBps` | 1000 (10%) | Percentage of raised quote for sZTR stakers |
| `sZTREnabled` | true | Toggle sZTR allocation |

With 800 WPROS raised and 10% allocation:
- **sZTR holders receive**: 80 WPROS (~$1,600)
- **Liquidity migration**: 720 WPROS + graduated tokens

## Transfer Restrictions

Tokens are **non-transferable until graduation**:

- Only whitelisted addresses (bonding, router, pair) can initiate transfers
- Users can sell (transfer TO whitelisted addresses)
- Full transferability unlocks after graduation

This prevents:
- Pre-graduation dumps
- Off-market trading
- Rug pulls before liquidity is established

## Parameter Tuning

### Impact Summary

| Change | Initial MCap | Capital | Grad MCap | Ratio |
|--------|--------------|---------|-----------|-------|
| ↑ K | ↑ | ↑ | ↑ | Same |
| ↓ K | ↓ | ↓ | ↓ | Same |
| ↑ assetRate | ↓ | ↓ | ↓ | Same |
| ↓ assetRate | ↑ | ↑ | ↑ | Same |
| ↑ Threshold | Same | ↓ | ↓ | ↓ |
| ↓ Threshold | Same | ↑ | ↑ | ↑ |

### Choosing Parameters

**For lower graduation market cap:** Increase threshold (but ratio decreases)

**For higher capital requirement:** Increase K (increases virtual liquidity proportionally)

**For same USD values with different quote price:** Adjust assetRate

## Comparison to Traditional Curves

| Aspect | Traditional Bonding Curves | ZentraFi Virtual AMM |

| **Model** | Linear/Exponential/Sigmoid | Constant Product (x×y=k) |
